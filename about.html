WHO AM I?

My name is Megan Helwig and I am a designer, illustrator and web developer living in Richmond, VA. I've lived in Richmond since I got my start as a designer with a Bachelor's from VCU's Robinson School of Media and Design in 2013. When I decided to commit to continuing to live in the city, I had the opportunity to work with a variety of small and unique agencies, studios and businesses.

My skill set and work ethic have lead me to gain experince in traditional and digital graphic design, illustration, screen printing, photography and photo editing, packaging design, social media content strategy/design, in-house design and most recently, web development and design. I recieved a full-stack web developer from the University of Richmond this summer, and have been working with Anthem as a Frontend Developer since July of this year.
