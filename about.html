WHO AM I?

My name is Megan Helwig and I am a designer, illustrator and web developer living in Richmond, VA. I've lived in Richmond since I got my start as a designer with a BA from VCU's Robinson School of Media and Design in 2013. The decision to stay in the city has given me the opportunity to work with a variety of small and unique agencies, studios and businesses.

My skill set and work ethic have lead me to gain experince in traditional and digital graphic design, illustration, screen printing, photography and photo editing, packaging design, social media content strategy/design, in-house design and most recently, web development and design. I recieved a full-stack web developer certificate from the University of Richmond this past July, and have been working with Anthem as a Front-End Developer since.
